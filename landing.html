How does it work after purchase?▼
After purchase you receive a direct link to the unlocked web app. No installation needed — it works in your phone or computer browser. Save the link to your bookmarks.
Does it work offline?▼
The map tiles require a connection. Descriptions and local secrets are loaded on startup. For areas with weak signal, open the app before you leave.
Will you add more regions?▼
Yes. Lazio is the first edition. Tuscany, Campania and Umbria are in progress for 2026. Early buyers will receive a discount on future regions.
How often are new places added?▼
We update each season with new places and content. All updates are free for existing buyers.
What devices does it work on?▼
Any device with a modern browser: iPhone, Android, tablet, Mac and PC. No installation required.
Is this only for Italian speakers?▼
No. The app is fully available in English and Italian. Switch language anytime from the top bar.
